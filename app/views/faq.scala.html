About building eZoomLayers

How to create quotes?
The quotes should be long (1-10 lines) and the summaries very short (1 to 5 lines). Allow yourself to choose the quotes subjectively: a mix between quotes that are essential to the story and quotes that you like (because of the language, the humor, the situation, the sharp eye of the narrator...). Do not systematically include summary lines between the quotes.

How to write summaries?
As needed for an understanding of the text, summarize the missing part between the quotes (the summary lines do not need to be systematically inserted). The summary lines are usually shorter than the quotes (for example one to 5 lines). The objective is to link one quote to the next through a summary; it helps to keep the progression of the text as a whole.

What document format should I useto upload the text?

What document format should I use to upload the text?

You have to use a .txt document, or you can create your eZoomBook directly on the platform.

The system requires the use of the following tags in order to build your eZoomBook :

* → Title of the text / eZoomBook

# → An introductory summary (several can be added, for example one for the content of the text, another to present the author).

% → A comment to add explanations (these comments will not appear in the final eZoomBook).

## → A summary to link the quotes.

** → A chapter or a section of the text followed by a title or subtitle (if it is an audio, you can use the timing to indicate the beginning of a section).

>> → A quote.

- None of the above tags needs to be placed to close the sections, the summaries, the quotes, the comments, etc. Simply put them at the beginning of each new element.

- Present it all in a single document (eZoomLayer).

How do I know if I can submit my layer, if its quality is good enough?

- When my work is finished, I check that I did not change the content of my quotations.

- I check that my summaries are written in the present tense.

- I know clearly who my pronouns refer to. If not, the names are indicated in bracket (ex: "He [David] was angry after Eddy.")

- When I read my full eZoomLayer, it makes sense on its own (without referring back to the original text).

- I swap my eZoomLayer with other members of the platform.

How can I help my partner improve his/her eZoomLayer?

- You can write suggestions that will be taken into account by the author of the eZoomLayer.

- Swap the text as many times as necessary to have the best result.

- You can give advices to your partner concerning the choice of the quotes, the content of the summaries...

- You can correct the morphology, syntax, and grammar mistakes.
